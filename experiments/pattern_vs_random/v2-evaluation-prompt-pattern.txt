=== ENGINE NOTES ===

- Action IDs: placement is `y * axis_size + x`. Action IDs that map to hole cells WILL raise an error.
  Use `engine.get_legal_actions()` — it already filters holes.
- `engine.topo.active_cells` is a list[int] of legal placement targets.
- `engine.topo.distance(a, b)` is graph distance over active cells (precomputed for all hole-bearing
  topologies via BFS). Manhattan would leak through holes; the engine uses BFS.
- Holes are walls: no piece, no influence (rules don't use influence here anyway), capture walks
  terminate at them, and connection paths must route around them.
- Connection win condition: P1 needs a same-coloured chain from x=0 to x=axis-1 (left-to-right);
  P2 needs y=0 to y=axis-1 (top-to-bottom). All four substrates are face-connected — connection
  is feasible on every condition.
